This week, an unexpected scripting anomaly returns the Moon to Earth's orbit, where it will inevitably crash into another slightly older Moon, killing everyone on the base. In the meantime, though, we get to experience a strange and elegiac meditation on having nowhere to belong and on the vagaries of inhabiting time. Inexplicable and haunting.
