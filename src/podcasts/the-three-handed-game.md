Celebrating the 1960s icon that is _The Avengers_, [_The Three Handed Game_]({{ url }}) selects triptychs of episodes based around specific themes of the 1960s and today. This is a commentary podcast best listened to with the accompanying episode on DVD, Blu-ray, or streaming. And, just to keep things neat, it's named after an episode of _The New Avengers_.
