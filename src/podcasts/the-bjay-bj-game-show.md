Bjay Hobbs and our own Brendan ‘BJ’ Jones invite you to play a new indie video game each month in [The Bjay BJ Game Show]({{ url }}). Each game they cover costs less than $20 and can be completed in under 20 hours, making it the perfect show for gamers with packed schedules, tight budgets, or huge game piles of shame. Or, let’s be honest, all three.
